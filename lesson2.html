Headings & Paragraphs

HTML headings range from <h1> to <h6>. Paragraphs are defined with <p> tags.

<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<p>This is a paragraph.</p>

Headings define the structure of content and improve accessibility. Paragraphs are used for text content.
