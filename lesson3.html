Lesson 3: Text Formatting & Semantic Tags

Bold, Italics, Span, Div, Header, Footer, Section, Article, Nav

Text Formatting

Text can be made bold or italic. Use <span> and <div> for grouping content.

<p>This is <strong>bold</strong> and <em>italic</em> text.</p>

Semantic Tags

HTML5 introduced semantic tags like <header>, <footer>, <section>, <article>, <nav> for meaningful structure.

Semantic tags improve readability, accessibility, and SEO by providing meaning to different parts of a webpage.
